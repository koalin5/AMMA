Selected Works

The Artist

Lucia Rossi

Born and raised in the bustling streets of Buenos Aires, my work is a direct reflection of the city's beautiful chaos. I deconstruct vintage magazines found in the Sunday markets of San Telmo, rearranging history to create new narratives.

Each piece explores the intersection of analog nostalgia and modern anxiety. Whether it's a physical cut-and-paste original or a digital composition, the goal is always the same: to find harmony in the fragmented.
